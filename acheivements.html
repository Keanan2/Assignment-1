SSLC ( Senior Specialist Leaders Course ) : Every year, the top 10 cadet leaders who have shown exceptional qualities in their National Cadet Corps (NCC) CCA will undergo a 3 day and 2 night residential camp at the NCC HQ to test their limits and develop their teamwork through military based activities to help them learn and obtain the skill set needed to lead their unit as NCC's future leaders. Through the completion of the camp, they will obtain the Staff Sergeant rank and come back stronger and mentally ready for the upcoming challenges of leading their school unit.

Outward Bound Singapore : Under the National Outdoor Adventure Education Masterplan, the MOE-OBS Challenge Programme seeks to develop ruggedness, resilience, and build cohesion amongst Secondary 3 students. The programme comprises a series of school-based Physical Education and Character and Citizenship Education lessons facilitated by teachers as well as a 5-day expeditionary course at Outward Bound Singapore (OBS).

Young Engineer Award ( YEA ) : The Young Engineer Award (YEA) is a certification by Science Centre Singapore and Singapore Polytechnic (School of Electrical & Electronic Engineering) to inspire secondary school students in STEM.
